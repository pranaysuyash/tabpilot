# Refund Policy

## Overview
This document outlines Chrome Tab Manager (TabPilot)'s refund policy for purchases made through our direct distribution channel.

## Refund Eligibility
- **Timeframe**: Refunds are available within 30 days of purchase
- **Conditions**: 
  - Purchase must be made directly through our website or authorized channels
  - Refund request must be submitted within 30 days of the purchase date
  - Only one refund per customer is permitted to prevent abuse
- **Exceptions**: 
  - Refunds may be granted outside the 30-day window for exceptional circumstances at our discretion
  - Fraudulent or suspicious activity will be investigated and may result in denial

## Refund Process
1. **Request Submission**:
   - Email refunds@tabpilot.app with:
     - Purchase email address
     - Purchase date (if available)
     - Order ID or transaction ID (if available)
     - Reason for refund request
   - Or use the refund request form on our website

2. **Verification**:
   - Our support team verifies the purchase in our payment processor (Dodo Payments)
   - We confirm the purchase is within the refund window
   - We check for any previous refund requests from the same email/payment method

3. **Processing**:
   - If approved, we initiate the refund through Dodo Payments
   - Refunds are issued to the original payment method
   - Processing time: 5-10 business days depending on payment method and bank

4. **Confirmation**:
   - You will receive an email confirmation with:
     - Refund ID
     - Amount refunded
     - Expected date of funds return
     - Information about license deactivation

5. **Post-Refund**:
   - Your TabPilot license will be deactivated
   - The app will revert to the free feature set
   - You may continue using the free features or uninstall the app
   - No further action is required on your part

## What Is Refunded
- The full purchase price of TabPilot
- Any applicable taxes that were collected
- Refund does not include:
  - Any optional donations or contributions
  - Third-party fees (though we absorb standard payment processor fees)

## What Is Not Refundable
- Purchases made through unauthorized third-party sellers
- Refund requests submitted after 30 days without exceptional circumstances
- Cases determined to be fraudulent or abusive
- Refunds for charitable donations or promotional credits

## Special Circumstances

### Technical Issues
If you're experiencing technical problems that prevent you from using TabPilot:
1. We will first attempt to resolve the issue through technical support
2. If the issue cannot be resolved and is verified as a bug in our software, a refund may be issued
3. We may offer an extended trial or future discount as an alternative

### Accidental Purchase
If you believe you purchased TabPilot by accident:
1. Contact us immediately with the details
2. If the purchase was made within the last 24 hours and the app has not been launched, we will typically honor a refund request
3. We understand mistakes happen and aim to be accommodating

### Dissatisfaction
We strive for customer satisfaction, but understand that software may not meet everyone's needs:
1. We encourage you to contact support to see if we can address your concerns
2. If the software genuinely doesn't work for your use case after attempting to use it as intended, we will consider refund requests
3. Simple preference change without attempting to use the software may not qualify for a refund

## Refund Abuse Prevention
To protect our ability to offer refunds to legitimate customers:
- We track refund requests by email, IP address, and payment method
- Multiple refund requests from the same source may trigger additional verification
- We reserve the right to refuse refund requests that appear to be part of a pattern of abuse
- Refund privileges may be suspended for users who repeatedly request refunds after using the software extensively

## Contact Information
- Email: refunds@tabpilot.app
- Website: https://tabpilot.app/refunds
- Response Time: We aim to respond to all refund requests within 2 business days

## Policy Updates
This refund policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting to our website. Your continued use of TabPilot after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the new policy.

This policy is effective as of March 26, 2026.
