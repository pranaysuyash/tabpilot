## Introduction
Chrome Tab Manager ("TabPilot", "we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our macOS application ("App" or "Service").

Please read this privacy policy carefully. By accessing or using the App, you agree to the terms of this privacy policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access or use the App.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide Directly
We may collect information that you provide directly to us, including:
- **Email address**: When you purchase TabPilot or contact support
- **Support communications**: Content of emails or messages you send to our support team
- **Feedback and reviews**: Any feedback you voluntarily provide about the App

### Information Collected Automatically
When you use the App, we may automatically collect certain information, including:
- **Usage data**: Information about how you interact with the App (features used, buttons clicked, etc.) - *only if you opt-in to analytics*
- **Technical information**: Device information, operating system version, App version, IP address (for security and update checking)
- **Crash logs**: If you opt-in to crash reporting, we may collect crash logs to improve stability

### Information We Do NOT Collect
TabPilot is designed with privacy in mind. We specifically do NOT collect:
- Your browsing history or Chrome tab contents
- The actual URLs or titles of your Chrome tabs (beyond what's necessary for duplicate detection processing)
- Personal files or documents
- Keystrokes or input outside of the App
- Screenshots or screen recordings
- Location data
- Contacts, photos, or other personal data from your device

## Data Processing and Storage

### Local Processing
The core functionality of TabPilot - scanning Chrome tabs, identifying duplicates, and managing tab closures - happens entirely on your local machine. Your tab data never leaves your computer during normal operation.

### Network Communications
TabPilot makes network connections only for the following purposes:
- **License verification**: Periodically checks your purchase status with our secure backend (if you purchased)
- **Update checking**: Checks for new versions of the App via our Sparkle update feed
- **Error reporting**: Sends crash logs if you have opted in to crash reporting
- **Usage analytics**: Sends anonymous usage data if you have opted in to analytics

All network communications are encrypted using HTTPS.

### Data Storage
- **Purchase information**: Your email address and purchase status are stored securely in our payment processor's systems (Dodo Payments or equivalent)
- **License verification cache**: A secure, encrypted cache of your license status is stored in your Keychain
- **App preferences**: Your settings and preferences are stored locally using standard macOS mechanisms (UserDefaults and application support folder)
- **Analytics/crash data**: If you opt in, limited, anonymized data is stored securely on our servers

## Your Choices

### Opting Out of Analytics and Crash Reporting
You can choose whether to share anonymous usage data and crash logs:
- Open TabPilot Preferences
- Navigate to the Advanced or Privacy section
- Toggle the settings for "Share anonymous usage data" and "Share crash logs"
- Changes take effect immediately

### Managing Your Email Preferences
You can opt out of receiving promotional emails from us by:
- Using the unsubscribe link in any promotional email we send
- Contacting us at privacy@tabpilot.app to request removal from our mailing list

### Accessing and Correcting Your Information
You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us at privacy@tabpilot.app. We will respond to your request within a reasonable timeframe.

### Do Not Track Signals

## Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information when you use our App or interact with our services. These measures include:
- Encrypting all network communications using HTTPS/TLS
- Storing sensitive data (like license verification tokens) in your Keychain
- Using industry-standard practices for our backend services
- Regularly updating our dependencies to address known vulnerabilities
- Limiting access to personal information to employees who need it to perform specific job functions

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.
